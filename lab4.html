Lab 4 - Tomato Bacon Grilled Cheese

A yummy meal that you and your kids will love! A simple golden brown grilled cheese sandwich with the twist of bacon and tomato. Hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 8 slices bacon
  • 1/4 cut butter, softened
  • 8 slices white bread
  • 9 slices American cheese
  • 8 slices tomato

Instructions: ingredients should be displayed as an unordered list, with no bullets. Meat ingredients should have a light red background color, vegetables should have a light green background, and dairy products should have a light yellow. The title should be in a sans serif font of your choice, and the instructions should have a heading “Instructions” and the font for the instructions should be italic.
